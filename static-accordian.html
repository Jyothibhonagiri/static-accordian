DIGITAL MEDIA

What is meant by digital media? +

A media analyst measures the effectiveness of a company's marketing campaigns through data research and evaluation reports. In these efforts, media analysts help companies maximize their brand and make smart marketing investments

what is the role of digital analyst? +

A digital analyst works to analyze marketing data related to online marketing, spotting trends, weaknesses, and opportunities in the volumes of online marketing data

What is digital analytics skills? +

Digital analyst skills empower you to decode trends and patterns in data while collating useful insights that prop up decision-making in businesses.
